Vedic Agni: The Cosmic Energy

by Sati Shankar

Abstract

This monograph explores the concept of Agni as the foundational cosmic energy, drawing from the Rig Veda and Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa. It is part of the series published by Vedic Hridaya Chakshu.
